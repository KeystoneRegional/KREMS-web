About KREMS

Keystone Regional EMS is a non-profit emergency medical services organization predominantly serving the Bucks County area. Established to provide dedicated medical response at community and public events, our organization plays a critical role in ensuring safety and emergency medical assistance during gatherings such as fairs, sports events, and public celebrations. KREMS is staffed by certified medical professionals including EMTs and paramedics who specialize in rapid-response and on-site medical care.
We operates with a fleet of QRS medical response units equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology to handle a variety of medical emergencies. Keystone Regional EMS is recognized for its efficiency and reliability in emergency preparedness and response, contributing significantly to the safety protocols of large-scale public and private gatherings. Our operational model focuses on readiness and fast deployment, which are crucial in managing potential health crises during large gatherings.
In addition to providing event-based medical services, Keystone Regional EMS engages in community outreach and education programs aimed at promoting safety and health awareness. These programs are designed to enhance community resilience and preparedness for emergencies.
KREMS provides invaluable support to events by offering swift and professional emergency medical services, enhancing safety and peace of mind for organizers and attendees alike. With highly trained personnel and state-of-the-art equipment, KREMS ensures rapid response and effective management of medical incidents, from minor injuries to critical emergencies. By having KREMS on-site, event organizers can mitigate risks, comply with safety regulations, and demonstrate a commitment to the well-being of their participants, creating a safer and more enjoyable experience for everyone involved.
